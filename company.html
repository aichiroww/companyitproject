Home

Visa is a multinational financial services corporation that facilitates electronic payment transactions around the world.

The company provides payment products and services to consumers, businesses, financial institutions, and governments in over 200 countries and territories.

Visa's payment network enables secure and convenient digital transactions using credit, debit, and prepaid cards, as well as mobile payments and other payment technologies.

Visa is a trusted world leader in digital payment technology that connects and enables individuals, businesses, and economies to thrive.

History of the company

Visa Inc. was founded in 1958 as BankAmericard, a credit card program for Bank of America customers in California. The program expanded nationally and internationally, and in 1976 it was renamed Visa. The company went public in 2008, and today it is listed on the New York Stock Exchange.
